Some notes about Glee Ams

Glee Ams is among the most popular music club for students in Vietnam. our aim is not only to create music, but also to create practical, meaningful values from music.

28th September 2018, I became vocalist of glee ams.
17th September 2019, I became president of glee ams.

let's go through my journey and view some of my works here.
